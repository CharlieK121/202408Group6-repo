Privacy Policy for Scholar Path

Effective Date: 10/18/2024

At Scholar Path, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our services. Please read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information when you use the Scholar Path application:

1.1 Personal Information - Name and Email Address: Required for creating an account and authenticating your identity. - University ID: Used to provide access to personalized campus navigation and course schedules.
1.2 Location Data - GPS Information: If enabled, we collect real-time GPS data to provide you with navigation assistance around the campus. You will have the option to enable or disable GPS tracking at any time.
1.3 Device Information - Device Type and Operating System: We collect non-identifiable information about the type of device and the operating system you are using to ensure that the application functions properly.
1.4 Usage Data - Log Data: We may collect log data such as your interactions with the app, time and duration of use, and features accessed. This helps us to improve user experience and troubleshoot any technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Information
Scholar Path uses your information in the following ways:

2.1 To Provide and Improve Our Services - Personal information such as your name, email address, and university ID is used to personalize your app experience, including displaying your class schedules and routes. - GPS data is used to provide navigation assistance to help you find locations on campus.
2.2 Communication - We may use your email address to send important updates, notifications, or changes to the services provided by Scholar Path.
2.3 Research and Development - Usage and log data are used to improve the app’s functionality, enhance user experience, and develop new features based on user feedback and app interaction patterns.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
Scholar Path does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:
3.1 Service Providers - We work with third-party service providers (such as hosting and cloud services) who assist in the operation of the app. These providers have access to the information needed to perform their services but are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.
3.2 Legal Compliance - We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the rights of Scholar Path, or ensure the safety of our users.

4. Data Security
We take your data security seriously. Scholar Path employs industry-standard measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption: All sensitive data, such as location information and login credentials, is encrypted both in transit and at rest. - Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal information, and strong authentication is used to secure that access. - Regular Audits: We regularly monitor and review our security practices to ensure that user data remains safe.

5. Data Retention
Scholar Path retains personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Users may request the deletion of their data at any time by contacting us (see Section 7 below).

6. Your Rights and Choices
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
6.1 Access and Rectification - You have the right to access and update your personal information at any time by logging into your account.
6.2 Data Portability - You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
6.3 Deletion - You can request the deletion of your personal data at any time. If you no longer wish to use Scholar Path, you can delete your account, and your data will be removed from our systems within [X] days.
6.4 Consent Withdrawal - You can withdraw your consent to data collection and processing at any time, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of the application (e.g., GPS-based navigation).

7. Changes to This Policy
Scholar Path reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users of any significant changes. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
By using Scholar Path, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
