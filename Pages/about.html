About Scholarpath

Scholar Path is a Web application that provides a campus map as well as directions for classes and other amenties on Campus
In addition Scholarpath also provides Amenties around campus as well as Study group organisation tools
Features include:
  • Campus Map Routing and directions
  • Building information and class room GPS locators
  • Food and Amentiies finder
  • Study Group organizer
